Our Story

We are undergraduate students at Purdue University that had enough with not being able to find vending machines! How many times have you been lost in campus, looking for a way to quench your thirst? How many times have you finally found a vending machine, only to find it has been out of order for the past 6 months? Well, we say this ends here, and no longer would it be difficult to find a vending machine!

Our Mission

We at Vending Network aim to make finding vending machines more accessible and convenient than ever before with our dedicated map of vending machines. Vending Network serves to create a personalized experience where one can customize their map for their own preferences and help each other from the Vending Network community in their vending-searching journey.
